About

Builderment is a game about automation, crafting, and production lines. Your goal is to build a factory to craft increasingly complex technology and ultimately a teleporter to supply Earth with infinite resources.

Features

  • Build factories on a massive scale
  • Utilize conveyor belts to transport items between factories
  • Craft dozens of unique items
  • Research technology to build more advanced buildings
  • Expand to harvest every resource on the map
